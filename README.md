# Historical Events Website Project

This repository contains our team’s initial proposal and planning materials for our historical events informational website. The project focuses on presenting major events in United States history in a clear, accurate, and educational format for students, educators, and lifelong learners.

## Project Overview
Our website will provide concise and in-depth information on key historical events such as World War I, World War II, Manifest Destiny, the Bill of Rights, and the Great Depression. Each event page will include causes, key figures, cultural and political context, and lasting impacts.

## Goals
- Present reliable and accessible historical information.
- Help users understand both the events and their broader significance.
- Create a clean, easy-to-navigate site using clear visuals and structured content.
- Support learning for students, teachers, and history enthusiasts.

## Target Audience
Students, educators, and general learners seeking trustworthy summaries of major U.S. historical events.

## Content Sources
Information will be gathered from textbooks, academic websites, and knowledge gained from coursework. All external sources will be credited appropriately in each page’s footer.
