Jax Fordham

Portfolio

Jax Fordham is an adjunct history professor at Souther Utah University. During his school life, he loved learning about all sorts of subjects before gaining an interest in history. Following his interests, he graduated college at SUU with a masters in history before returing to become a professor at his alma mater. While he is a fan of all history, he has specialized in teaching history in the 1900's.

He is currently engrossed in a project to teach American history with his fellow coworkers: William Barker, Jason Maggio, Jaden Marciel-Shapiro, and Zach Maggio.
