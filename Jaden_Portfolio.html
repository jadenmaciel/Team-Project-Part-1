About Me

I'm an early-career cybersecurity professional working in FinTech while earning my B.S. in Cybersecurity (GPA 3.87) with a Computer Science minor at Southern Utah University.

In my role as an ISV Support Specialist at Buzz Financial / ExpiTrans, I help software partners integrate safely with our payments platform, review KYC/AML documentation under FinCEN/FINTRAC/BSA guidelines, and maintain audit-ready records.

I also contribute to a multi-tenant shipping microservice (Python/FastAPI, Docker, AWS/GCP) integrated with the Troute payment gateway, where I work with secure patterns like JWT/HMAC validation, tenant scoping, and containerized services designed for future SIEM visibility.

My passion lies in building secure, scalable systems that protect sensitive financial data while maintaining compliance with regulatory standards. I'm constantly learning and staying current with the latest security threats and defense mechanisms.
